Who We Are

Teensocket is a group of teens who are dedicated in coding and teaching.Teensocket was founded by Bankole Ahmed and Habeeb Omotosho.It was founded on the 21st of October 2018. TeenSocket was built for teaching and developing.We teach other teens and childrens how to be independent by learning how to code,build,create and think.We beleive that we can make a change in the lives of young teens in Nigeria and other parts of the world. Our team consists of a group of professionals which are web developers,software developers,andriod developers,game developers and graphic designers.Through this,we help educate others who don't have the priviledge to learn programming. We hope to influence the lives of other teens and children who can help shape the future and be among the best of the best.
